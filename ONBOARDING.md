## Team Tips

- **Autonomous-first** — Design every task to keep running with minimal human input. Aim for work that continues even when no one is at the keyboard.
- **Token-efficient by default** — Spend tokens in service of the goal. Don't read whole files or run broad searches when a targeted check will do.
- **Goal over polish** — Tests, refactoring, extensibility, and stability hardening are lower priority. Get the objective done first; improve later.
